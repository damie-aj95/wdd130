Splash Wild Water Rafting

Our mission is to provide unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that combine thrilling adventure with the highest standards of safety. We believe that nature's rivers offer the perfect backdrop for creating lasting memories, building teamwork, and discovering the joy of outdoor adventure. Every trip is designed to challenge and inspire our guests while respecting the natural beauty of the waterways we navigate.

History

Founded in 1995, Splash Wild Water Rafting began with a simple dream: to share the exhilarating beauty of whitewater rivers with adventurers from around the world. What started as a small family operation with just two rafts has grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting companies. Over the past three decades, we've guided thousands of guests down pristine rapids, trained dozens of expert guides, and maintained an impeccable safety record. Our commitment to environmental stewardship and community involvement has made us not just a business, but a vital part of the river conservation movement.
